Hello and thank you for stopping by.

My name is Galina Malkova. My family and I came to the United States from Belarus (Europe) five years ago. Moving here has been one of the biggest challenges in my life.

I liked making handcrafted items since I was a child. I am able to do various kinds of needlework, including sewing, knitting, crocheting, cross-stitching, scrapbooking, decoupage, handcrafting jewelry, and etc., and I often use several of them at once while working on an item.

I prefer sewing and creating unique handmade items which keep the warmth and inspiration of mine. Handcrafting inspires me and helps me feel more comfortable in a new country. Who knows, maybe, one day my hobby will become my job.

I would appreciate any comments/reviews regarding my work.

Best Wishes,
Galina.
